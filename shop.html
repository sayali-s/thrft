Your Go-To List Of Instagram Thrift Stores In India


I. Bombay Closet Cleanse
Bombay Closet Cleanse sells items such as kurtas and masks, they also have 100 per cent charity sales where all the profits go to a particular cause. Sustainable, great fashion and for a cause? That’s a triple win if you ask us.

II. Carol’s Shop & Tea Room
With handpicked collectables from around the world in their bag, Carol’s Shop & Tea Room presents an array of delicate as well as bold options to choose from. Their Instagram feed is complete with pieces of art and mesmerising photographs.

III. Copper Boom Vintage
An aesthetic appeal of their own, Copper Boom is beside the ordinary fashion. With unusual prints, style and materials, their clothing items deserve an in-depth look. Their latest live chat describes the working of an Instagram thrift store in detail.

IV. Findings
Curated Findings definitely lives up to their name with their awe-worthy accessories, as well as home decor items. Their core values are re-wear, repair, re-create recycle and re-love.

V. Disco>ery Culture
Influenced by the disco scene in the 70s and the 80s, Disco>very has you covered with jackets, jerseys and so much more. The culture of the past reflects in all their pieces, and how.

VI. Lulu Thrift
This Manipur-based thrift store with its funky and appealing edits offers no particular style, or all and has a little something for everyone. They accept Google Pay and bank deposit.

VII. Slow
The brand engages directly with vendors and mills in Surat and Mumbai, collecting sampling waste, end-of-roll textiles, and second-hand textiles like saris and bedsheets; upcycling that which needs some TLC. Look out for vintage pieces from Balenciaga, Ralph Lauren, Ann Taylor (a lot of it!), Liz Claiborne, Van Laack and Alfred Dunner in their latest curation. They accept Google Pay.

VIII. Folkpants
The bulk of their edit comes from stores (in their hometown or beyond) specialising in rare vintage or vintage-inspired pieces. Their pre-owned designer labels include Vetements, Kenzo, Chrome Hearts and Hanae Mori among others.

IX. Mumbai Thrifts
If tie-dye cropped sweatshirts and light-washed denim with unicorns on them sound like your cup of tea, Mumbai Thrifts is your destination, with so much more in store. They accept Google Pay and online fund transfer.

X. Aimée
Your search for thrifted outerwear ends with this Insta-store run by Vaibhavi Javalkar and Akshay Tambe. The founders’ stylist friends constantly help grow their collection, which is also made up of gems found on their various travels. Look out for elevated separates, outerwear and loungewear from brands like Fendi, Diane von Furstenberg, Free People, Pierre Cardin and La Perla.
